Date Cookies:

Ingredients:
1/2 pound dates
1 stick of butter
1 egg
1 tbsp milk
3/4 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups rice krispies

Directions:
Melt butter, add dates and sugar
Bring to rolling boil and allow to boil around a minute
Remove from heat and let cool 2 minutes
Add vanilla, then egg and milk and mix until combined
Add rice krispies
Allow to cool until mixture does not burn your hands
Roll into small balls, then roll in walnuts
